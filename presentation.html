Why We Built This
The Problem
🔄

Generic apps don't adapt to you

Same plan for a beginner and an advanced athlete. No personalisation, no injury awareness, no goal intelligence.

No signal at the gym? App shows a spinner. Your workout is lost. Not acceptable.

Your data owned by corporations

Accounts, subscriptions, servers. Your personal health data sold and breached.

Zero AI intelligence
